Welcome to Marina's Journey Blog!

Meet Marina!

Born and raised in Veles, Macedonia, Marina is one of those people who left their comfortable home at young age, to travel the world and learn about life. Her style is to stay in one place she likes for 3 months (or more) every year, to know what it feels like to eat, cook, speak and sleep in another culture that isn’t hers.

Work and Travel

Most of the time, in order to find accommodation more easily while staying in different countries, she gets a job and stays with a work visa. According to her, working in a different job, in a different country, with different nationalities of colleagues and clients, is the best way to experience the spirit of the country she visited.

Let's go on andventure, together!

What happens if I go out there on my own and work for myself, don't share anything and travel the world alone? I am still doing that, but in this blog, I will share with you all the joys, successes, and struggles of this life adventure, so I hope i'll motivate someone to start a new life experience!
